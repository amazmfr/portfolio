Sandwich packaging & logo design: "Natrue"

The assignment was to create a logo for a food brand named "Natrue" and design 3 packages. The first is a sandwich packaging called NaTrue POP for a bacon and cheese flavour, I wanted to create a sandwich packaging that stood out using bright colours and colourblock. The second is another sandwich packaging, This time for their vegan sandwiches. This follows a similar colourblock pattern to the POP version but in green. The final packaging is for their kettle-cooked chips. I used more earthy colours to give a sense of artiginality.
