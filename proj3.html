Omotenashi house logo design

These are 2 designs for the 2012 Solar Decathlon contestant; the Omotenashi house. This project begins with an analysis into the Omotenashi house and how it functions. This was a fascinating dive into the world of architecture, I love projects where I get to research and discover more about what I am designing. The assignment was to design 2 logos based on an element so I chose the theme "house of water".
